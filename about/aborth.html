What is the Abort Feature?

PLEASE NOTE: Your tab will still be able to be seen with GoGuardian and other software, if you want evasion from that, open our game pages, or use Bunker. This is intended to allow more freedom with tabs while still providing solutions for privacy.

To put it short. It's a way of evading unwated supervision quickly, and easily.

Activate the abort feature via the Settings tab, and then just check the box, and hit submit

Abort will convert your tab to something non-suspicous when you switch the tab, or view another window.

If you want to go back to your original tab, just hit the back button.

This will allow you to evade unwated attention from remote software, and physically. This is an alternative to about:blank, which allows users more freedom with their tab, while still hiding it when they need to.
